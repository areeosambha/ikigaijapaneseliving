What is Ikigai?

Ikigai (生き甲斐) is a Japanese concept meaning "a reason for being." It is what gets you out of bed in the morning. A combination of passion, vocation, profession, and mission, it represents a perfect balance between what you love, what you're good at, what the world needs, and what you can be paid for.

The idea is deeply rooted in Okinawan culture, where people live long, happy lives. Researchers have identified Ikigai as one of the core factors contributing to the longevity of Okinawan centenarians.

Core Principles of Ikigai

  • Start small (Kaizen approach)
  • Release yourself (Let go of your ego)
  • Harmony and sustainability
  • The joy of little things
  • Being in the here and now (Flow state)

Practicing these principles helps align your inner purpose with your external life, creating a more fulfilling and balanced lifestyle.
